About

Macy is 22 years old and is currently attending Brigham Young University. She comes from a family of 7, where she is the second of 5 kids. She is originally from Bountiful, Utah and is a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. She is spontaneous, energetic, driven, adventurous, happy, and friendly.

Growing up with a doctor as the head of the house, she developed a passion for learning about the human body. In addition to studying it formally, she also loves to experiment with exercise, diet, and other wellness activities. You can often find Macy on a run, playing a sport, or reading a book about the body.

Tennis

Macy followed in her mom's footsteps and developed a passion for the sport of tennis. She stepped on the court for the first time at age 6 and immediately fell in love. She started playing competitvely when she started her freshman year of highschool, and went on to win two state single titles. She continued her tennis career at Colorado Mesa University.

Hiking

One of Macy's greatest passions is exploring the great outdoors. She has summitted peaks in the Utah and Colorado areas, including some over 14,000ft high. She loves to explore new trails and is always up for a hike.

Skiing

In the winter months you will catch Macy on the ski slopes. She has skied for as long as she can remember, and thinks there is nothing better than a fresh powder day. She loves skiing with her friends and family, and rarely goes alone. Her favorite ski resorts in Utah include Snowbird, Brighton, and Snow Basin.

Learning

Macy loves to learn, and has found great joy in her formal education. She graduated high school in 2021 and will graduate from Brigham Young University in 2026. She is pursuing a degree in Exercise and Wellness. Her first two years of college were completed at Colorado Mesa University, before she transferred the BYU to finish her degree.

Travel

Macy loves to travel. She is always looking for her next destination and trying to convince her friends to come with her. One of her favorite ways to travel is with non-proift organizations or doing humanitarian work. She has recently worked with HXP and WWCRF.
